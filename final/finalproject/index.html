here's a collection of my favorite stores, online and in-person, to buy earrings!

  • Amoeba Clay. 2 of my earrings(the green one and the one on the far right) are both from here. While I bought these at the Davis Street Market, you can now buy her work online, on Etsy.
  • Fern & Vine Handmade. 2 earrings on this page (the black ones with gold stars/moon/flecks) are from this store. They're available on Etsy, and they're the store that got me into clay earrings.
  • Flower Dangles. My first foray into clay earrings. I have 2 earrings from this store, and now that she's back from a hiatus, I plan on buying so much more.
  • Davis Farmer Market, Davis Street Market, Pence Gallery and Neighborhood Market 530 all have different sellers with unique, handmade jewelry! If not online, I love buying jewelry from local markets in whatever city I'm in.
